5 Pet Care Myths You Should Stop Believing 🤯

🚫 Don't fall for old pet tales! We're busting the internet's most common myths about cats and dogs. 🐶🐱 Learn what’s fact and what’s fiction — and discover smarter, safer ways to care for your furry friends. 💕

🐾 Busting the Biggest Pet Myths!

1. 🐕 A Cold, Wet Nose Doesn't Mean Health

❄️ While often a good sign, a wet nose doesn't guarantee good health. Body temperature and hydration are better indicators — a sick dog can still have a cold nose, while a healthy dog’s nose might dry out after a nap.

2. 🦷 Kibble Doesn't Clean Teeth

🍪 Dry kibble is like crackers — it crumbles instead of scrubbing! Dental chews, daily brushing, and professional cleanings are the best ways to prevent plaque and tartar buildup.

3. 🥛 Cats Need Milk? NO!

🚫 Most adult cats are lactose intolerant! Giving them milk can cause stomach upset, vomiting, or diarrhea. 💧 Water is the healthiest (and safest) drink your cat needs.

4. 🧠 You *Can* Teach an Old Dog New Tricks!

💪 Absolutely true! Older dogs often learn faster than puppies because they’re calmer and more focused. Training keeps their brains active and strengthens your bond. 🐾

5. 😝 A Dog’s Mouth Isn’t Cleaner Than a Human's

🦠 Dog saliva has bacteria made for *dogs*, not people. It may contain E. coli or salmonella — so while dog kisses are cute, they’re not exactly germ-free! 💋🐶
