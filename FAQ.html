General Questions

FurEver Friends is your one-stop online resource connecting animal lovers with top-rated pet shops, salons, and a community for general pet welfare information. We aim to make finding the best for your pet easy and enjoyable!

For site issues, feedback, or general inquiries, please navigate to the "Contact Us" page and send us a message through the provided form. We aim to respond within 24-48 business hours.

Currently, FurEver Friends primarily serves specific regions. Please check our "Services" page for a list of areas where our listed shops and salons operate.

Pet Shop & Products

No, FurEver Friends acts as a directory and platform. We list trusted, external pet shops. All product purchases, shipping, and return policies are managed directly by the individual pet shop you choose.

Each shop's policy will be available on their respective website, which you can access via the links on our Services page. We highly recommend reviewing their policies before making a purchase.

While FurEver Friends doesn't directly offer discounts, many of our partner shops and salons may have their own promotions. Check their individual pages or sign up for their newsletters for deals!

Pet Salon & Grooming Services

Many of our listed salons are highly experienced in handling anxious, elderly, or special needs pets. We strongly recommend calling the salon *before* booking to discuss your pet's specific requirements and ensure they can provide the best care.

The fastest and most reliable way to book an appointment is usually by calling the pet salon directly. Some salons also offer convenient online booking through their own websites.

For popular salons or during peak seasons (holidays, summer), it's advisable to book at least 1-2 weeks in advance. For regular maintenance, a few days' notice is often sufficient.
